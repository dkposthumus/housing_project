– Planning, Building Codes Amendment – Ordinance, sponsored by Supervisor Ronen, 
amending the Planning and Building Codes to increase fines and penalties for violations of 
Planning and Building Code provisions; clarify that violations affecting more than one unit 
in a building constitute multiple violations for purposes of assessing penalties; requiring 
the Planning Commission and the Historic Preservation Commission to adopt factors for 
the Zoning Administrator to consider in determining the appropriate amount of civil 
penalties; establishing penalties for residential units merged, constructed, or divided 
without required permits or approvals; establishing penalties for violations involving 
illegal demolition and enhancement of penalty amounts for certain buildings by age or 
historic status; providing additional notices for Responsible Parties; affirming the Planning 
Department’s determination under the California Environmental Quality Act; and making 
findings of consistency with the General Plan, and the eight priority policies of Planning 
Code, Section 101.1, and findings of public necessity, convenience, and welfare under

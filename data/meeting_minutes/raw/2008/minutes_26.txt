1b.2007.1342K(K. GUY: (415) 558-6163)
168-186 Eddy Street- southwest corner at Taylor Street, Lots 010 and 011 of Assessor's Block 0331 -Request to considerwhether the shading or shadowingon Boeddeker Park from the proposed Eddy and Taylor Family Housing Project will not be significant or adverse, pursuant to Planning Code Section 295 (The Sunlight Ordinance). The proposed project would demolish an existing surface parking lot and construct a new 14-story mixed-use building containing approximately 178 affordable dwelling units and ground-floor retail space. The project site is located within the RC-4 (Residential-Commercial Combined, High Density) Zoning District, the North of Market Residential Special Use District (Subarea 1), and the 80-130-T Height and Bulk District.
Preliminary Recommendation: Adopt the Motion finding that net new shadow from the project is not adverse, and authorizing the allocation of the Boeddeker Park cumulative shadow limit to this project.
SPEAKERS:None
ACTION:CEQA review is not complete and the joint commissions did not hear or consider this request

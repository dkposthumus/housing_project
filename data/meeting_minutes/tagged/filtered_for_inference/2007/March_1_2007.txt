A.
 
 
EXECUTIVE SESSION
 


 


PLANNING DIRECTOR SEARCH
 
- The Planning Commission will meet in closed session pursuant to San Francisco Administrative Code Section 67.10(b) and Government Code Section 54957(b)(1) to discuss candidates for the position of Director of Planning.


 


 
 SPEAKERS during the public comment period prior to going into closed session:


 
 
Marilyn Amini




It is very important that the public be part of this process.


The new director should be one who values process and requirements and not just efficiency.


Requested a public hearing with the candidates before forwarding names for the Major's consideration.




 


At the conclusion of the closed session, and pursuant to Section 67.10(b) of the San Francisco Administrative Code, the Commission will reconvene in open session and shall by motion and vote elect to:


 




Disclose no information, or




 




Disclose information that a majority deems to be in the public interest.




 


Following is the action taken by the Commission during the public period of the meeting following closed session:
 


 


 
 ACTION: Disclose no information


 
 AYES:
 
 
 
 Antonini, S. Lee, W. Lee, Olague and Sugaya


 
 ABSENT: Alexander and Moore


 


 


Adjournment:
 
 
2:25 p.m.


 


THESE MINUTES WERE PROPOSED FOR ADOPTION AT THE REGULAR MEETING OF THE PLANNING COMMISSION ON THURSDAY, MARCH 15, 2007.


 


 


SPEAKERS:
 
 
 
 None
 


ACTION:
 
Approved
